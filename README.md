# 👻 Ghost Hunter

A fast-paced, spooky web game where your mission is simple: **hunt down as many ghosts as you can before the clock runs out!** 🎯

🔗 **Live Demo:** [Ghost Hunter Game](https://syntax-surfer-1.github.io/Ghost-Hunter)  
📂 **Repository:** [GitHub Repo](https://github.com/Syntax-Surfer-1/Ghost-Hunter)

---

## 🎮 About the Game

Welcome to **Ghost Hunter** – a fun, fast, and frightfully addictive browser game.  
Ghosts appear randomly across your screen – your job is to catch them with a quick click or tap.  
But hurry... you’re racing against the clock! ⏰

---

## ✨ Features

- 👻 **Random ghost appearances** to keep things unpredictable  
- 🖱️ **Simple click-to-catch mechanic** – easy to learn, hard to master  
- ⏱️ **Timer-based gameplay** – see how many you can catch before time runs out  
- 🎮 **Spooky, Retro UI** for immersive fun  
- 📱 **Responsive design** – works smoothly on desktops, tablets, and phones
